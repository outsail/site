Requesting an Eprom Reprogram

I can reprogram Eproms or create replacement chips for most GM cars 86-93, using your engine code if you break a pin or somehow break yours. Every year/model of car has a different set of things that can be modified in the Eproms, Because of this I am unable to tell you what I can do on your car without knowing a few things first. The easiest way to find out is to fill out the form at the bottom of this page and I can check it out and tell you if I would be able to do the things you are asking for. Just fill one of these out with your email at the end and I will send you out an email when I have an answer for you.

What can I do?

Again, it depends on the vehicle, but some of the most common things are,

  • - VATS disabled
  • - EGR disabled (and check engine light removal)
  • - Speed Limiter disabled
  • - Fan on/off Temp Modification
  • - Injector size modification
  • - Disable specific Check Engine Codes
(For basic modifications, such as these listed above, I charge $40 + shipping)

How it works

You are responsible for the cost of shipping, so after we decide what can be done you ship the eprom/memcal from your ecu to me. Once I get it I will reprogram it and will give you a choice of shipment options to send it back. you will pay me the cost and the shipping charge via PAYPAL then I ship your Eprom/memcal back.

Of course this means the amount of time you are without your Eprom/memcal depends on how much you want to pay for shipping, it takes me 1-2 days to actually program the Eprom/memcal once I receive it.
